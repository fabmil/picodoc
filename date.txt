date
====



Imposta data e ora
------------------
# date MMDDhhmm



Emette data e ora
-----------------
$ date "+%Y%m%d%H%M%S"



Emette da 00:00:00 1970-01-01 UTC (a GNU extension)
---------------------------------------------------
$ date "+%s"



Emette la data in formato DD-MM-YYYY
------------------------------------

$ date '+%Y-%m-%d'
